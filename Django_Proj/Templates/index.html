What is music ?

Music is the as the art of arranging sound to create some combination of form, harmony, melody, rhythm or otherwise expressive content. Modern music is heard in a bewildering profusion of styles, many of them contemporary, others engendered in past eras. It lends itself easily to alliances with words, as in song, and with physical movement, as in dance.

Are Genres important?

Genres are important as genres often dictate what a musician puts out and what the audience expects from it and often tells a history of that music's development. Clearly a lot of music doesn't fit in boxes so easily but a lot of music often does. Often people stick to favorite genres and become quite knowledgeable in them, trying every release. Other's may stick with just the classics of a specific genre and not branch out any further.

PleasantSound brings ..

This project uses AI model to predict the genre of music. The file can be uploaded in the classify section. We bring the intersection of music and technology.
